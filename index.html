About Our Company

We are a Kenya-based general supplies and construction firm driven by quality, trust, and results. With a team of seasoned experts, we deliver cost-effective solutions across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our Journey to Excellence

Founded in 2016, Cycon Invested Ltd started with a single truck, a small team, and a big vision — to offer dependable general supplies and construction services. Through hard work, integrity, and consistent delivery, we’ve built a strong reputation for quality and reliability. Today, we are proud to be a trusted partner for homeowners, contractors, and institutions across Kenya.

With over 8 years of experience, Cycon continues to grow — driven by client trust, professionalism, and a commitment to excellence in every project.

What we specialize in

Comprehensive solutions for all your construction and supply needs

Borehole drilling process

We offer borehole drilling services from site survey to water testing. Our team uses modern equipment to drill clean, deep, and reliable water sources for homes, farms, and businesses.

General supplies

We provide fast and reliable delivery of construction materials using well-maintained tipper trucks. Whether it’s sand, ballast, murram, or hardcore, our tippers ensure safe and timely transport right to your site. .

Residential Construction

From custom homes to renovations, we deliver exceptional residential construction services with attention to detail and quality craftsmanship.

More Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your construction and supply needs

project management

We offer end-to-end project management to ensure your construction runs smoothly, stays on schedule, and meets your budget. From planning and coordination to supervision and reporting, our team handles every stage professionally — delivering quality results with zero stress for the client. .

site preparation and groundwork

We provide complete site preparation services including land clearing, grading, levelling, and excavation. Our team ensures your site is safe, stable, and ready for construction — saving you time and reducing future risks. .

Commercial Construction

We specialize in commercial construction projects such as offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and institutional buildings. From foundation to finish, Cycon Invested Ltd delivers durable, functional, and cost-effective solutions tailored to your business needs.
